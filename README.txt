# Chillie Tote - Business Website for Tote Bags

## Description
Chillie Tote is a business website dedicated to offering a wide variety of stylish and eco-friendly tote bags. Our mission is to provide customers with high-quality, durable, and trendy tote bags that suit all occasions, from casual outings to shopping trips. This website showcases our products and allows users to easily browse and purchase their favorite totes.

## Features
- Comprehensive product catalog with detailed descriptions and images.
- Shopping cart and checkout functionality.
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop users.
- Secure payment integration for easy online purchases.
